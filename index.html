About Us

CT Premed Consulting is a team based group that works to provide you multiple perspectives on the path to medical school. Choose the packages that suit you and your needs best and we will work to ensure that you know what you need to do to become a competitive medical school applicant.

We have packages that can help everyone from high school students to people in the process of applying to medical school. Our group’s combined experience includes insight of the medical application process from premed students that successfully matriculated to medical schools, extensive experience conducting medical school interviews, and insider’s keys from serving on admissions committees.
